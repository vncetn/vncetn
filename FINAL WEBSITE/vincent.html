About Me

I am Vincent Corpes Jr., 19 years old, currently pursuing Bachelor of Science in Computer Science at West Visayas State University. I am the founder of binssaps.

I love engaging in photography, editing, and layouting, finding joy in capturing moments, refining images, and creating visually compelling layouts.

The photos I've shared serve as my sole means of self-expression at the moment, encapsulating the essence of who I am and the experiences that define me.

Purpose of this website

  • Displaying my distinct photography style and creative vision.
  • Compiling a collection of my best work for personal satisfaction and growth.
  • Easily sharing my photos with loved ones, allowing them to see my passion.
  • Joining online photography communities and connecting with fellow enthusiasts.
  • Gaining insights and encouragement from others who appreciate my work.
  • Using the website as a platform to experiment with new techniques and styles.
  • Building a digital diary of moments captured, reflecting on my photographic journey.
  • Experiencing a sense of achievement as I curate and share my photographic journey.
